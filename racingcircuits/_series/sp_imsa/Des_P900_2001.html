LE-MANS SERIES
2001 Season

Prototype 900 Category

 

The Prototype 900 class is comprised of two divisions; Le Mans Prototype (LMP) and the Grand Touring Prototypes (GTP). The two types of cars are classified together in ALMS races.

 

LMP- Closed-wheel, purpose-built racing chassis. Limited to 90 litre fuel tanks, six forward gears, 6.0 litre normally aspirated or 4.0 litre turbocharged engines, carbon fiber brake discs and 16 inch wheels. The cars, except in specific cases, may weigh no less than 900 kilograms.

 

GTP- Low-volume, production-based, two-wheel drive cars formerly known as the "GT1" class. Limited to 90 litre fuel tanks, five or six forward gears, carbon fiber brake discs and 14 inch wheels. The cars, except in specific cases, may weigh no less than 900 kilograms. Unibody constructed cars are permitted to compete with normally aspirated engines up to 8.0 litres in displacement and turbocharged engines up to 4.0 litres.
