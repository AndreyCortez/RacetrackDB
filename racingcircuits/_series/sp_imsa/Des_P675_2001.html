LE-MANS SERIES
2001 Season

Prototype 675 Category

 

The cars cannot weigh less than 675 kg, but are similar to LMP 900 cars in most specifications. Engines are limited to 3.4 litre (normally aspirated) and 2.0 litre (turbocharged). The cars use carbon fiber brake discs that can be slightly smaller than LMP 900 cars, and 14-inch wheels.

 

Information provided from the American Le Mans Series Official Website
