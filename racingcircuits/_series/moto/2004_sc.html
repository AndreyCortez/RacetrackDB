The original schedule for the 2004 Sidecar World Championship was abandoned when the organisers were unable to negotiate an agreement with the promotors of the World Superbike Championship.

Later in the year, the FIM announced that a 'World Cup' was to be awarded to the best overall points scorers from a three race event at Schliez.

In the absence of a World Championship schedule, the UEM European Championship was promoted as the premier sidecar category for 2004.
