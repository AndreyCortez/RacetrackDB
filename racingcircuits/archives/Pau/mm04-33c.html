14 - Heading to the Casino, Avenue Alfred de Musset (a 100/200 m section that you cannot drive clockwise today).


15 - Very fast esse of the Parc Beaumont.


16 - Maréchal Foch statue.


17-A - Foch complex. The most difficult corner. The braking zone is in the preceding long right curve. It's a left-right esse, but the road is bumpy because here the circuit begins to go downhill. The apex of the right curve is invisible (just in the middle of the photo), and is marked by a cone during races (see the red light). Foch statue stands on the left.


17-B - Foch complex, at the apex of the left curve.


17-C - Foch complex, at the apex of the right curve.
