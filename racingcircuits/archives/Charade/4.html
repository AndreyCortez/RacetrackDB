#048 - Gravenoire. One of the most exciting and difficult curves in the world, which can be compared with "l'Eau Rouge" at Spa or better with the "Fuchsröhre" at the Nürburgring... Just imagine a 1972 Formula 1 car flat out in this curve....what a thrill!! You couldn't loose speed in this curve because the road was going uphill hard from this point...


#049 - Ivor Bueb lost his life after crashing in this curve. He is the only driver to have died during an event at Charade.
