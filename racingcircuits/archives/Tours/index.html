Tours, France

Type: Street Circuit
Length: 14.180 Miles / 22.819 km
Used: 1923
Photographs Taken: July 2004

The circuit consisted of a triangle of over 14 miles of public roads between the towns of La Membrolle and Semblançay, located to the north-west of Tours. The circuit was only used in 1923, hosting the UMF Motorcycle Grand Prix, the Grand Prix de Tourisme for light cars and finally the French Grand Prix, which was won by the Sunbeam of Henry Segrave.
