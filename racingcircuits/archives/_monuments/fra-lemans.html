Motor Sport Monuments and Memorials - Le Mans, France

Photo ©Chris Hall


This is the memorial stone to Sebastien Enjolras, the 21 year old WM driver who died in practice for the 1997 24-Heures du Mans. You can make out the shape of his car at the top of the stone.

The stone itself is situated on the right hand side of the road about ¼ mile after Arnage corner. The two photos beneath the map show the view back and forward from the site.
