Motor Sport Monuments and Memorials - Montlhéry, France

This is a Montlhéry monument built on the right side of the road climbing to the track, at a crossroad, at about 500m before the circuit gate. Not easy to see.


Photo ©2004 Michel Marti
Front side. The inscription reads:
English Translation

DEUX GRANDS
CHAMPIONS DE
LA COURSE
AUTOMOBILE

TWO GREAT
CHAMPIONS OF
MOTOR RACING


Photo ©2004 Michel Marti
Right side of the Montlhery memorial.
English Translation:

GEORGES BOILLOT
VANQUIEUR de GRAND PRIX de L'ACF
EN 1913 et de NOMBREUSES ÉPREUVES
MORT POUR LA FRANCE
le 19 MAI 1916

GEORGES BOILLOT
WINNER of the ACF GRAND PRIX
IN 1913 and NUMEROUS RACES
DIED FOR FRANCE
19th MAY 1916
