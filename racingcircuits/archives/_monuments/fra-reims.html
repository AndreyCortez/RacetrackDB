The Charles Faroux memorial plaque is located at the front of the small grandstand on the old start-finish straight of the Reims circuit in France.
English Translation:

IN MEMORIAM
CHARLES FAROUX
1872   1957
PRESIDENT DE L'ASSOCIATION DES JOURNALISTES SPORTS
DIRECTOR DES GRANDS PRIX SUR LE CIRCUIT DE REIMS

IN MEMORIAM
CHARLES FAROUX
1872   1957
PRESIDENT OF THE ASSOCIATION OF SPORTS  JOURNALISTS
DIRECTOR OF GRANDS PRIX ON THE CIRCUIT OF REIMS
