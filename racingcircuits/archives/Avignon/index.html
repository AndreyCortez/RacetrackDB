Avignon, France

Type: Undeveloped Street Circuit Proposal
Photo's Taken: 4 August, 2005

Originally scheduled to host a round of the 2005 DTM series from the 16th to 18th September, this street circuit was to be located alongside the river Rhône. However. the organisers were unable to attain the necessary legal permission to host a street race, and consequently the race was instead held at the Eurospeedway Lausitzring.
