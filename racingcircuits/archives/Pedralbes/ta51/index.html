#28 - César Azpezleguía (Ecurie France - Talbot-Lago T26C)
Apezteguía had arranged to loan this car for the race from Paul Vallée, co-owner of Ecurie France. He drove it during practice, however Vallée then sold the car to Australian Harry Dale before the race and poor Apezteguía could not use the car he had entered.
