Further Details
There where races held in Brasilia already in 1960. The route was different from the 1962 & 66-70 courses, but I couldn't find much more detail apart from it being mainly held on roads at the southern side of the central bus terminal.
The 1962 layout contains some nonsensical corners coming of the main N/S highway at the north end of the loop. At least in current day Brasilia it is impossible to just turn left there. I couldn't find any period photos that showed that area of the town, so I went with what every other map of the layout shows.
