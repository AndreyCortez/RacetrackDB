Further Details
The track has been undergoing renovations since 2015. This has been a very stop start procedure, hence the amount of proposals. In 2024 it is still under reconstruction, with satellite images pointing towards the 2022 proposal.
I don't know when the Kart track was opened. Sources say 2003, but it is already on photos dating back to 2002. Sadly that is the earliest I could find evidence for it, although it may be older than that.
