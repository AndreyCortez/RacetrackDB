Further Details
Since the track is essentially a car park with some extra dirt loops added, the layouts can vary a lot. Most of the layouts shown were reconstructed using GoogleEarth’s historic images. While the temporal coverage is good, the temporary nature of the layouts means I’ve most likely missed some.
The track dates back to before 2010. Za Rulem magzine mentions the track being rebuild in the late 2000, but nothing further is known about the earlier variant of the track.
Racing in the snow appears to be a regular occurance at the track. The only winter layouts I’ve been able to positively confirm are the 2017 and the 2014 ones. As far as I can tell the 2017 layout is the one in current use.
The Autocross layouts are still in operation past 2018, but due to their temporary nature non of them are visible in the GE snapshots.
