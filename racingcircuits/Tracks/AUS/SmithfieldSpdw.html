Further Details
The tracks location is based on the plot of land mentioned in the lease agreement and the cadastral map of the Hundred of Munno Para
I have not found any maps or pictures showing the track clearly enough to determine its shape. According to sources Strathalbyn Speedway was a copy of Smithfield, alas no pictures of the former have turned up either. Smithfield's length is given as 1 mile, with fairly large radius turns, it was located on a 1/3 mile square plot of land. That would indicate a track with large turns and short straights to me, although that is pure speculation.
