Rupauna Speedway at Motorsport Memorial
Various Motocross/Scramble tracks are visible in the aerial survey images all through the 1970s and 1980s. Primarily west of T1 and east of the speedway. Due to the ephemeral nature of these tracks they are not included in the maps
1970s extension & 1979 MX layout based on traces in survey image 5468_N_1. Graeme Cornelius confirmed initial cuts being made for the extensions, but was unsure about details regarding MX track.
The history of the early RC track is unkown it appears first on images sometime between 1975 and 1980.
