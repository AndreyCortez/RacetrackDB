When was the small ~3/16th mile oval build. The photos I've access to put it between 1992 and 97. How long did it operate?
What is the banking angle for the Highbanks Speedway?
I have no clue how jet boat tracks are laid out. So the track shown here is just the waterways, not the actual route taken in the race.
Mid 2000s Google Earth images show two or three tracks north of the dragstrip. They seem to have been in use for some years, futher information on them is requested.
When was the oval north of the dirt track build? Satellites photos put it between 1997 and 2008.
The dirt tracks websites mentions an addition of an offroad courses in the infield of the track. I couldn't make out any infield changes. All that keeps changing on the dirt track over the years is the final corner.
There may have been a MX track to the south of the Highbanks in the mid 80s. Traces show only show up in the 1986 aerial survey, so it may have been short lived.
The 2002 entry is based on the changes between the 1997 and 2008 aerial photo. The only definite change in 2002 is the permanent establishment of the jet boat course, and the final change to the dirt track. Everything else new in the 2002 image may have been build sometime between 1998 and 2007.
