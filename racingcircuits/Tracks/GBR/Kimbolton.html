Further Details
The 1959 track is based on a track map from 1988. I’m unsure if that was the original layout or not. There is little information on the web about the track prior to the mid 80s, during which the layout appears to be the same as the one depicted.
The early layouts were a temporary course marked by haybales. It’s doubtful the 59 layout is the correct one. Unfortunately there is little information on the early years available.
