Further Details
1989 Kart track shows up in the 1993 images. Pressumably it was build during the big rebuild in 89, unfortunately the 89 image set doesn't cover the area.
I'm unsure how long the Club circuit's shortcut was in use. It appears that it was abandoned by the mid 2000s alongside the inner pitlane.
New Karting pitin appears between 2008-10.
The original kart track got converted to parking over the years, but still seems to have a groove until quite late. I'm not sure when it was abbandoned as a track.
The track website mentions a motocross track in the 1960s. The track shown is my best guess. It roughly fits the the stated length and explains some odd tracks in the infield.

The track's website says the Club track was build in either 2000 or 2003. The first pictures that show it are from 2002, while it isn't there in 2000s. Hence the 2001 date for its inaugural year on the page.
