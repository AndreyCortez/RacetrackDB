Further Details
The roads used consisted of everything from dirt to paved roads.
The 1895 race started at Versailles and finished at Porte Maillot in Paris. The Paris-Bordeaux races always started at Versailles
Usually the races were neutralized when the cars passed through towns. This is not shown on the map, as no information specific information could be found on the neutralization zone's exact locations.
The cancelled Bordeaux Madrid leg of the 1903 races was reconstructed using the concurrently held tourist drive. This event largely followed the race route, but was more freeform in terms required checkpoints. Hence the route shown here may vary to some degree from the one laid out for the race. The Spanish leg of the race is largely undocumented so any information on its route are highly welcome.
