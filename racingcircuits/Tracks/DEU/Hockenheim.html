German Grand Prix Winners
The track was renamed to Kurpfalzring during the 1938 rebuild, only one race meeting was held under that name before WW2 broke out. Post war the track resumed its original name.

Build dates and operational years are highly uncertain. Dates given are primarily based on Google Earths historic images. Any clarifications on dates are highly appreciated.
The 1964 proposal appears to have plans for multiple layouts in and around the new Motodrom section. The layout shown here appears to be one of many that were planned. It's unknown if any where ever used for competition or at all.
The supermoto layout is one of many similar variants since 2004. The tarmac portions are of temporary nature and vary from event to event. The layout shown is only representative of the general shape of the Supermoto track.
