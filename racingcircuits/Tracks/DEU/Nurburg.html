Further Details
I'm uncertain when the Steilstrecke was last used for a motorrace. Indications are that it was some time in the 1960s.
The Betonschleife was build together with the rest of the facility in 1927, but first used as a standalone track in 1931
The pitlane was separated from the track in 1969
The track underwent major renovations in 1971, with several kinks taken out and crests eased. Arguably that warrants its own entry in the chronology, but I couldn't find a comprehensive list of changes. I'm not even sure the changes would show up in the scale the Nordschleife layouts are depicted here. For now the Nordschleife is based on the post 71 track until I can get my hands on the original blueprints.

There is a claim that in 1973 to slow the entrance into Kallenhard, a left was added after Metzgesfeld. I could not find any indication that happened. Problem is that coverage of that area is sparse during the early 1970, so I can't be totally certain this is a wrong claim.
