Racingsportscars gives two different lap lenghts for the roadcourse for 1959 and 1960 respectively. The difference in distance roughly matches the elimination of the Hook section. Although it could be just a more accuret measurement of the original layout.'
Ascarate Speedway and Evans Oval are most likely the same track. With the track moving to Enchanment Park when the golf course was build at Ascarate.
There is a small 1/10th mile track inside the roadcourse in the mid 60s photos. I couldn't find out what it was used for, although there is mention of a kart track belonging to the amusement park.
