Further Details
I'm unsure when the track opened. I found programs dating back to 1969 or 70, while Lost Dirt Tracks and Speedway and Roadrace History say the track was opened in 73.
1975 USGS survey image appears to show an infield track, possibly for motocross. I'm unsure if it was still in operation at the time the image was taken.
Dragstriplist says there were drag races held at the track. I can't find any loaction that remotly looks like a dragstrip on the property.
