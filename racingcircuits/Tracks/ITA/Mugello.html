Further Details
The early races were held on mostly unsealed surfaces, hence the prewar layouts are shown as dirt roads.
The motocross layouts and timeline is a best guess.
The 1966 proposal is scaled to the right size, based on the distance given by G.de Carli. It's orientation is based on the topography of the area.
The distances for the road course vary by up to 5km for no apparent reason. If as stated that the layout didn't change between 1914 and 1965, the loss of 5km of road between the early 1920s and 1928&29 races may indicate a minor change in route.
Autodromo Mugello at Motorsport Memorial.
Mugello Road Circuit at Motorsport Memorial.
Parts of the Piccolo Mugello Circuit including the starting area were submerged when Lake Bilancino was created in the late 1990s.
Google Earth shows, two Kart circuits appearing in 2011. Due to a gap in coverage I can't tell when Kart B was established, but it falls into disuse after 2011/13. I assume it was a stopgap before the more permanent Kart track was build, question remains when exactly was it first used?.
Start locations for the road coursed may be slightly out of place. I had a hard time matching photos to loaction on the maps.
