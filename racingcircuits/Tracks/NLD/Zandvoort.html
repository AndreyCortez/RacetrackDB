Further Details
1948-88 Layouts are based on WW2 aerial recon photos. The Scheivlak to Hondenvlak section isn't well defined in them, thus the version shown is a best guess based on onboard footage and oblique aerial photos from the mid 70s.
Panoramabocht and Marlboro chicane suffer from a similar lack of sources.
It is unknown how long the Beerenpotsteeg short circuit was used. The shortcut was paved back in the 1960s, but sources only mention it being used as a racetrack in the 1980s.
It is unknown when the interior Kart track was build, but it appears in track maps through out the 1990s.
Rob Semmeling mentions a 1978 proposal to modify the circuit. I'm lacking the plans to show it here.
