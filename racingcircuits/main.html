IMPORTANT ANNOUNCEMENT - CLICK HERE

This site contains details and contact information for motor racing circuits throughout the world.

Circuits are classified by continent and country in alphabetical order.

To access a circuit's information, click on it's continent in the top frame to activate a list of countries in the side frame. Select the country from the side bar to bring up a list of circuits. All information for a circuit can then be displayed by clicking the circuit name.

If a circuit has multiple layouts or previously used a different configuration, then information about these variants can be obtained by clicking the relevant hyperlink underneath the circuit map.

Circuit information for all of the major international motor racing series can also be obtained by clicking the 'Racing Calendars' button in the side bar and choosing the relevant year from the right hand side of the screen. This section also contains details of race winners and series champions where available.
